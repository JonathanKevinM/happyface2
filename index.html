About the Artist

Hi, I’m Faith — a décor artist and spatial stylist crafting immersive celebrations across Kenya. I blend floral artistry, modern props, and lighting design to create scenes that feel personal and unforgettable.

Over the past 5+ years I’ve styled weddings, ruracios, birthdays, corporate launches and cultural ceremonies. My process is collaborative: mood‑boarding, site visits, 3D mockups when needed, and meticulous execution on the day.
